Bed & Breakfast and Spa Rooms

The Victorian Room

The Victorian Room has a Queen Size Orthopedic mattress with pillow top for the best sleep. Your bedroom is decorated with soft pastels, floral duvet and lace curtains to enjoy the view of the garden. There is an ensuite (private bathroom in the room) and a TV with a PVR for your comfort. Air Conditioned.

The Egyptian Room

The Egyptian room is a dynamic statement. The focal point of the room is an Egyptian pictograph of the "Myth of Maat". Your bedroom has direct access to the Hot Tub. The Queen Size Orthopedic mattress with pillow top will contribute to a very restful sleep. There is an ensuite (private bathroom in the room), and a TV with a PVR for your comfort. Air Conditioned.

The House

The entire main floor is dedicated to the BB and Day Spa (Air Conditioned). There are two parlors to enjoy a good book and music. The Fireplace room has leather sofas to enjoy complimentary sherry. The formal parlor has big overstuffed chairs and sofa to curl up with the sun shining through the picture window. All guests have access to the hot tub. As part of your stay a bathrobe, slippers and a fresh towel is provided to each guest.

Free High-Speed Internet

Guests receive free high-speed Internet access by connecting PCs to our wireless network and/or using our guest laptop computer.

Day Spa

Create a more relaxing time by taking advantage of the Day Spa . For an additional fee you can include massage, and pampering (mud wrap, body polish and herbal wrap).

Your massage therapist will listen carefully to what you hope to get out of your massage. She will give you the chance to tell her how much pressure you prefer, areas you may want to focus on, or any injuries/tight spots you may have. Also the comfort and modesty of each person is of utmost importance. You will be covered with a sheet throughout the whole session and only the body part receiving treatment will be uncovered.

Kitchen and Barbecue

Our kitchen and backyard barbecue are available free of charge for all bed and breakfast guests. At An English Bed & Breakfast, you can cook your own food instead of eating at restaurants for every meal. You are also welcome to keep your food and beverages in the refrigerator.

Hot Tub and Decks

Along with the hot tub there are two decks, one open to Alberta's sunny skies and the other is covered. In the back garden there is a fire pit to roast your favorite food and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.
