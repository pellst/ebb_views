An English Bed, Breakfast & Day Spa

We would like to invite you to spend your holiday in Calgary enjoying An English Bed, Breakfast and Day Spa Experience. We have customized our home to provide you with optimal comfort (Air Conditioned) and privacy.

The entire main floor is dedicated to the B&B and Day Spa. There are two parlors to enjoy a good book and music. The Fireplace room has leather sofas to enjoy complimentary sherry. The formal parlor has big overstuffed chairs and sofa to curl up with the sun shining through the picture window. All guests have access to the hot tub. As part of your stay a bathrobe, slippers and a fresh towel is provided to each guest.

Guests have complete access to use our kitchen area (year-round) and backyard Barbecue (seasonal).

Guests receive free high-speed Internet access by connecting PCs to our wireless internet network and/or using our guest laptop computer.

Each morning is accompanied by the fresh aroma of a gourmet breakfast. Teri creates a memorable culinary experience. Please let us know of any dietary restrictions when you book.

Located in Northwest Calgary with easy access to the airport, we are on the way to Banff and Lake Louise. The home is fifteen minutes from downtown, two blocks from great restaurants, shopping and the LRT (Light Rail Transit). Canada Olympic Park, McMahon Stadium and The University of Calgary are also nearby facilities. Calgary has a network of biking/jogging pathways throughout the city that are accessible from our location.
