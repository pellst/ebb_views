Located in Northwest Calgary with easy access to the airport, An English Bed, Breakfast & Day Spa is on the way to Banff and Lake Louise. The home is fifteen minutes from downtown, two blocks from great restaurants, shopping and the LRT (Light Rail Transit). Canada Olympic Park, McMahon Stadium and The University of Calgary are also nearby facilities. Calgary has a network of biking and jogging pathways throughout the city that are accessible from our location.
